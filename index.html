About Me

I am Sandra Ananti, a data scientist with a background in statistics and a strong passion for solving problems using data-driven approaches. With hands-on experience in data analysis, machine learning, time series forecasting, and interactive dashboards, I specialize in transforming raw data into actionable insights that support informed decision-making.

Beyond technical expertise, my work experience across government, private organizations, and NGOs has strengthened my ability to communicate insights effectively, work with diverse teams, and apply data science to real-world challenges.
